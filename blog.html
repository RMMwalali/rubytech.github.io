HOW TO START A WATER VENDING BUSINESS



1.Location to set up a water vending machine.


Consider a highly populated location and research on the community water suppliers.
Thinking of getting a vending machine but not sure if your property is a good fit?
The list below covers the best places where vending operators like to install their equipment:-
1. Apartment communities.
2. Hotels.
3. Near manufacturing facilities.
4. Offices.
5. Retail stores.
6. Auto shops.




2.Make sure you have an adequate supply of water from the vendor.

To start a business you have to have a water supply either from a borehole, county water or any other source of water.
This analysis of water will determine the components necessary to purify the water completely.




3.How to make profit using the water vending machine.

Profit, like any retail business your profitability is directly related to your sales.
If you can put your water dispensing machines in a very busy location with high demand for your product you will make significant sales.
If you can sell your product at a price where you can maintain a decent margin then you will make profits.
