WATER VENDING MACHINES

WATER VENDING MACHINES
- Water vending machines (ATMs) are made of a coin operated reverse osmosis system.
- This makes it easier to supply clean water in public and private places at affordable prices.
- Water ATMs can be installed at:
  • 1. Bus Stops.
  • 2. Public and Private Places.
  • 3. Railway and Metro Stations.
  • 4. Hospital and Nursing Homes.

BENEFITS
* It's cost effective and economical for both urban and rural areas.
* There is also a complete guarantee of safe and clean drinking water which frees people from worries of water-borne diseases.
* It dispenses safe and pure water all year round and enables people to purchase water on a 'pay oer use' basis.
* Saves time and effort that people spend in travelling or standing in queues for clean water.
* Reduces the dependence of citizens on packaged drinking water, which is otherwise expensive especially in the rural areas.

COIN OPERATED WATER ATMS
- We are engaged in manufacturing a wide range of water vending machines made from corrosion resistant stainless steel and are protected from dust and bacteria.
- We make all the machines as per client requests.
- Coin acceptor ATMs can be installed at:
  • 1. Schools.
  • 2. Public and Private Places.
  • 3. Railway and Metro Stations.
  • 4. Hospital and Nursing Homes.

BENEFITS
* Our machines are low maintenance hence higher profits can be acquired.
* Clean water can be accessed at all hours of the day at affordable prices.
* We customise all the machines to the water type and chemical composition.
